Regulatory Decision Summaries

ADEMPAS

Medicinal ingredient
Riociguat

Therapeutic classification
Soluble guanylate cyclase (sGC) stimulator

What was the purpose of this submission?

With this submission, Adempas was approved for the treatment of:

  • inoperable chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension (CTEPH, WHO Group 4);
  • persistent or recurrent CTEPH after surgical treatment; in adults (= 18 years of age) with WHO Functional Class II or III pulmonary hypertension.

Why was the submission approved?

Health Canada considers that the benefits of therapy with Adempas outweigh the potential risks.

For more information on Health Canada's decision, please view the Summary Basis of Decision


Additional Information

Prescription status
Riociguat is listed on the Prescription Drug List and therefore Adempas will be available by prescription only.
